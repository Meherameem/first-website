About Alan Rickman

Early Life

Alan Sidney Patrick Rickman was born into a working class family in Acton, West London.His father was a factory worker, house painter. decorator and former WW2 aircraft fitter. His mother was homemaker. Rickman had two brothers and a sister.As a child, he excelled at calligraphy and watercolour painting. Rickman attended Derwentwater Primary School in Acton, and then Latymer Upper School in London through the Direct Grant system, where he became involved in drama. After leaving Latymer with science A Levels, he attended Chelsea College of Art and Design and then the Royal College of Art. After graduation, Rickman and several friends opened a graphic design studio called Graphiti, but after three years of successful business, he decided that he was going to pursue acting professionally.

Career

Alan Rickman's first breakthrough was the role in The Barchester Chronicies.Rickman played a wide range of roles-romantic, villians, television role.Rickman took issue with being typecast as a villain, even though he was known for playing "unsympathetic characters".
